Products and Platform Specialist

Adam joined the company in 2017 and looks after the Nadex platforms and product offering. It’s a role he enjoys on a professional and personal level – with an active interest in trading, Adam’s been a retail trader himself for more than 10 years. Adam graduated from Tulane University in 2013 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Finance. Prior to joining Nadex, he spent more than two years working as a financial analyst. His experience spans major financial services and real estate, with a focus on personal and business lending.
