Nadex Weekly Charts & Analysis - Feb. 22

Bryan Caprice / Nadex markets
Bryan Caprice /Nadex markets
February 22, 2022
12:00 pm ET
1 hr

Join us to get a rundown of what’s going on in the markets right now and what to keep your eyes on for the rest of the week. Bryan Caprice, CEO of Keep Trading Simple, and Todd Rich, Head of Education at Nadex, share live analysis of various markets and how to potentially turn that into opportunities using Nadex Binary Options, Call Spreads, and Knock-Outs.

During this session, Bryan and Todd will look at the week ahead highlighting upcoming news announcements across the globe. They'll discuss key levels for both news breakouts as well as standard price action, and then highlight different potential trade set-ups leveraging the Nadex trading platform.
