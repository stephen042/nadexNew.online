18 new contracts available to trade – new opportunities on Nadex

  • Adam McAden
September 22, 2020
We’ve launched eight new knock-outs and ten new call spreads, available to trade now in our platform. Learn about them and the new opportunities they present.

We’re excited to announce the launch of 18 brand new Nadex contracts, giving you more of what you love and bringing you additional opportunities on the markets. We’ve introduced eight new knock-outs and ten new call spreads, available to trade now on the Nadex platform.

New Nadex Knock-Outs

You can now trade eight new knock-outs based on forex pairs and commodities:

  • AUD/USD

  • USD/CAD

  • EUR/GBP

  • AUD/JPY

  • GBP/JPY

  • EUR/JPY

  • Natural gas

  • Silver

We picked some of our most popular underlying markets for our new knock-outs. When traders tell us what they enjoy and ask for more contracts, we like to deliver.

New weekly Nadex Call Spreads

Weekly call spreads are worth up to $750, making them some of the largest dollar value contracts on the exchange. This opens up opportunities if you’re looking for higher profit targets.  

Weekly call spread durations are available now for the following 10 forex pairs:

  • AUD/USD

  • AUD/JPY

  • EUR/USD

  • GBP/USD

  • USD/JPY

  • USD/CHF

  • USD/CAD

  • EUR/GBP

  • GBP/JPY

  • EUR/JPY

More opportunities on the markets you love

These new knock-outs and call spreads form part of our ongoing commitment to building out our offering, bringing traders like you new opportunities. We strive to provide more optionality, with contracts that will suit different trading styles and strategies.

Contracts based on the Australian dollar are some of our most popular – we’re particularly excited to present new knock-outs and call spreads based on this currency.

Get trading our new knock-outs and call spreads

Head on over to the platform and take a look!

Don’t have an account yet? Sign up for a demo account and try out our new contracts for free, along with all our existing binary options, knock-outs, and call spreads. You’ll find educational resources on our site to help you get started and build your confidence.
